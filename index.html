About Us

Govt. Model Higher Secondary School Sumbal is a government institution located in Sumbal, Bandipora, Jammu & Kashmir. The school has been officially structured as a Higher Secondary institution since 1981 and provides education for classes 9th to 12th.

Vision

To create educated, responsible and disciplined citizens.

Mission

  • Provide quality education
  • Maintain discipline
  • Promote academic excellence
  • Support overall development

Principal Message

Our mission is to provide quality education and strong moral values, helping students grow academically and personally.
