About Us

Govt. Model Higher Secondary School Sumbal is located in Sumbal, Bandipora, Jammu & Kashmir. Established in 1981, it provides education from class 9th to 12th with focus on discipline and quality education.

Vision

To create educated, responsible and disciplined citizens.

Mission

  • Provide quality education
  • Maintain discipline
  • Promote academic excellence
  • Support overall development

Principal Message

Our mission is to provide quality education and strong moral values, helping students grow academically and personally.
